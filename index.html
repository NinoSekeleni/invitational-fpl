★ The Fine Print ★

Rules
& FAQ

Everything you need to know before you put your R350 down for The XII. Fair, simple, and the same for everyone.

01
How you win

The Invitational runs on the official FPL classic league. Whoever has the most total points across the 38 gameweeks at season's end is the champion. 2nd and 3rd are the next highest. A separate Head-to-Head league runs alongside, and its winner takes the H2H prize.

02
Entry & eligibility

One FPL team per manager. The R350 entry must be paid by the deadline to be eligible for any prize. Your name shows as Pending until payment lands, then Paid. Anyone can join — your full FPL history is imported so you're ranked from day one.

03
The prize pot

The pot is built from entries at R350 each. The published split — 1st, 2nd, 3rd and the Head-to-Head winner — is based on a full house of around 30 managers. Final amounts are confirmed once registration closes and the field is set.

04
Deadlines

Registration and payment close on —. No entries after the deadline, and no refunds once the season is under way. Get in early — places are capped.

05
Chips & conduct

Standard FPL rules apply — all chips (Wildcard, Bench Boost, Triple Captain, Free Hit) are allowed as per the official game. Play your own team, no shared accounts, and keep the banter in the group chat clean-ish.

06
Tie-breakers

Level on points? The official FPL tie-break applies (most goals scored by your players, then fewest transfers). Prize money for tied places is pooled and split equally.

Do I need to have played before?
No. Your entire FPL record is imported when you join, so you arrive with a real ranking in the Hall of Fame — late starters are never behind on day one.
How do I pay?
EFT — banking details are shared once you register via WhatsApp. Reference your name so we can mark you Paid.
What if numbers change?
The entry fee and prize split are fixed for The XII unless announced otherwise. If the field is well under 30, the commissioner may adjust the pot proportionally and tell everyone before kick-off.
Who runs it?
The league is founded and run by Nino Sekeleni. Questions, disputes or payment proof go to him directly on WhatsApp.

How it's scored. Points are the official FPL season totals from each contestant's history. The leaderboard ranks by cumulative points within the chosen window; not everyone played every season, so Avg/Szn levels the field for those who started late.
